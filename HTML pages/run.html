What is it?

‚Ä¢ It is a runner game and the my first ever project in Unity.
‚Ä¢ The player has the goal to reach 500 points, after which the game ends. The score points increase based on the distance and the speed of the player's cube.
‚Ä¢ The player will face enemy cubes in his path, the enemies will also shoot at the player. An enemy bullet does 2HP of damage, while colliding with the enemy decreases 15HP from the total of 100 Player HP.
‚Ä¢ There are also abilities such as Speed and Heal. Heal ability heals up the player to full 100HP while speed boosts the movement speed of the player for a limited time.
